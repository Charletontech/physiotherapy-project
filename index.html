Our Services

Medical Massage

We offer expertise Medical Massage services. Medical massage can help in physical relaxation, improved joint mobility, improved blood circulation (which enhances oxygen and nutrient supply to cells), reduce inflammatory conditions like arthritis, lower blood pressure, relieve from pain and other health benefits.

Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation

Our Physiotherapy services helps our clients in speedy recovery from injuries and body stiffness, increase the mechanical funtionality of various parts of the body, correct postural deformities, improve overall strength and coordination, its also reduces dependency on medicines.
We also help our clients recover from back and neck pains, sports injuries and osteoporosis.

Chiropratic therapy

Chiropractic therapies focus on the back, it involves physically adjusting the vertebrae in your back. This therapy is particularly helpful in cases of neck and lower back pains, headaches or stress.
Chiropractic therapy is used to treat scoliosis - a condition where there is an abnormal curvature of the spine which can cause pain and reduce your range of motion.

Speech Therapy

Our speech therapy services provides treatment and surpport for people experiencing speech disorders and communication problems. speech disorders can develop in multple ways. They can be due to nerve injuries to the brain or even muscular paralysis. Speech disablities lead to conditions such as stuttering, Aphasia and articulation disorders.

Mental Therapy

Mental (or occupational) therapy helps people of all ages overcome challenges completing everyday tasks or activities - what we call "occupations".
We help people living with mental health conditions , physical or learning disabilities, birth injuries or birth defects, sensory processing disorders, injuries to the brain or spinal cord, autism, broken bones or other orthopedic injuries to undergo fast paced recovery from these challenges and improve the quality of their lives.

Dietetics Consultation

Let us help you live a nourished and healthy life by placing you on a diet plan specially schemed to fit your body needs. A well-balanced diet provides you with energy and the nutirents you need for growth and repair, making you strong and healthy and help to prevent diet-related diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, obesity, renal disease and food allergies.

Stress Management

Many mordern day jobs impacts a lot of stress to the body. for instance bankers who sit from morning till evening, straining their spinal cord.
Massage helps relieve stress by lowering the heart rate, increasing relaxation and increasing feel good hormones. Anxiety and depression can be greatly reduced by maasage therapy.
